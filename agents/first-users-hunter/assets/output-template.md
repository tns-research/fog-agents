## Methodology

- Channel discovery via `exa search` + `firecrawl scrape` on community indexes (Reddit, Slack directories, Discord lists, Lu.ma, IndieHackers).
- Active-community gate: ≥10 unique posters/week, last thread <48h, replies-per-post >2.
- Channel scoring: audience fit × ease of access × expected conversion (each 1-5).
- LinkedIn-adjacent profiles surfaced via `exa search` on public pages (no scraping behind auth).

- Private Slacks / gated Discords cannot be verified for activity from outside; flagged "unknown" rather than dropped.
- LinkedIn-adjacent profiles are public-page snapshots only; no Sales Navigator data.
- IRL events list reflects the next 6 months as visible at scan time; verify dates before showing up.
