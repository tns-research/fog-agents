## Methodology

- GSC data window: last <N> days vs prior <N> days, country filter `<code or all>`.
- SERP scrape via `firecrawl scrape --type=html` for top 10 organic per target query.
- Decay thresholds: -30% clicks over 2 weeks, -2 positions on high-value queries, -25% CTR with stable position.
- Fix prioritization: `impact × ease`, both 1-5.

## Limitations

- GSC sampling: queries with <10 impressions are filtered by Google and absent from the data.
- SERP results captured at one point in time, not personalized to the user's geo.
- AI Overviews / featured-snippet citation is captured only if SERPER.dev is configured; otherwise this surface is not audited.
